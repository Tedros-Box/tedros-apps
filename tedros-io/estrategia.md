Currently, the site is too technical. We need to pivot the messaging from "How it works" (Java/Framework focus) to "Business Value" (Profitability/Efficiency focus) to attract **IT Companies, Software Houses, and Outsourcing Providers**.

# Objective
Rebuild the landing page structure and content to position Tedros Box as the ultimate productivity platform for IT service providers who serve the private and public sectors.
